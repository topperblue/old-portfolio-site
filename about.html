About Me

My name is Adam and I am a programmer with a love for solving puzzles. I have always been inclined to make sense of the world around me using logical reasoning. When I discovered the world of programming as a young teenager, this inclination became a passion. Ever since then, I have become increasingly more involved with computers until I found myself pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science.

I created this site to showcase some of my work as a programmer and other endevors! Check out the "A-Frame" page for an interactive VR recreation of a Keith Harring painting. The "Projects" page will show you more of web-based projects I have worked on. The "Sounds" page lets you listen to some of the music I have made.
